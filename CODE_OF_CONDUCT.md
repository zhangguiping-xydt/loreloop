# Code of Conduct

LoreLoop follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Be respectful, constructive, and considerate. Harassment, discrimination,
threats, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project
spaces. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation
to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer contact listed in the
repository security or community profile. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical. Until a dedicated conduct team exists, the active
maintainers jointly handle reports; a maintainer named in a report must recuse
themself.
